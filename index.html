FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the program

What if I miss a lesson?

+

Lessons can be rescheduled within the program timeframe. We understand life happens, so we offer flexible scheduling to ensure you don't miss out on any content.

Can I take more than 6 lessons?

+

Absolutely! Additional lessons are available at R50/hour. Many students opt for extra sessions to dive deeper into specific topics or get more practice.

Do I need trading experience before joining?

+

No experience necessary! The program is designed for complete beginners. We start with the fundamentals and progressively build your skills to professional level.

What happens if I lose money in Month 2?

+

If your account balance drops below R600, you will be obligated to reimburse the full amount of R600. However, you'll still receive full mentorship and guidance to learn from the experience. Many successful traders faced initial losses before finding consistency.

What equipment do I need?

+

A computer or laptop with internet connection is essential. A smartphone can work for some lessons, but a computer is highly recommended for the trading platform in Month 2.

How long are the lessons?

+

Each core lesson is one hour long, focused on a specific topic. We keep them concise and actionable so you can absorb the material without information overload.
